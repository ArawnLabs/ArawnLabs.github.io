Introduction

Thank you for playing Inversia ("we", "our", "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile game Inversia. Please read it carefully.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We take a minimal approach to data. To be clear:

  • We do not collect, store, or share personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
  • We do not request or require access to sensitive device permissions (camera, microphone, contacts, precise location, etc.).
  • We do not collect precise location data from users.

If you believe we have unintentionally collected personal data, please contact us immediately so we can investigate and remove it.

2. Third‑Party Advertising

Inversia displays ads from third‑party advertising networks (for example, Google AdMob). These third parties may collect or process device or usage information to serve ads and may use technologies such as cookies or advertising identifiers.

Important points:

  • We do not control the data collection or use practices of third‑party ad networks. Their privacy policies govern their use of data.
  • Some ad networks may use identifiers (such as the Advertising ID on Android) to deliver interest‑based or personalized ads. To learn how Google handles ads data, review Google's advertising policies.
  • If you wish to opt out of personalized advertising, you can do so through your device settings or ad network controls where available.

3. Age

Inversia is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 provided personal data through the game, contact us and we will promptly delete any such data.

4. Security

Because we do not store user personal data, we have limited data security obligations. Nevertheless, we take commercially reasonable measures to protect any information related to the app's operation and to maintain the integrity of the game.

5. Your Rights

Because we do not collect personal information, you generally do not have data access, correction, or deletion requests to make with us. If you have questions about how a third party (such as an ad network) processes data related to your use of Inversia, you should consult that third party's privacy policy and exercise rights directly with them where applicable.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we do, we will update the "Effective date" at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
